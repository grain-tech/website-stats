Campaign Retrospective

Summary

The Flavour Personality quiz ran for 52 days (8 Jan – 28 Feb 2026). It generated 1,695 page views and 431 completions at a 56% completion rate. 95 users shared their results, primarily via Instagram Stories and WhatsApp.

What worked
  • Strong viral spike on 20–21 Apr drove 3x normal traffic — likely triggered by an Instagram Story reshare
  • High completion rate (56%) suggests the quiz was engaging once started
  • "Creature of Comfort" was the most common result (201), giving insight into the customer base
  • Mobile-first audience (55%) validated the decision to design for mobile screens
  • Direct traffic dominated (63%), meaning QR codes and word-of-mouth worked
What could improve
  • Only 45% of visitors started the quiz — the landing page needs a stronger hook
  • Share rate was low (22% of completions) — consider adding incentives or making the result card more share-worthy
  • Instagram feed drove only 12% of traffic vs. 38% from Stories — invest more in Story-format promotion
  • "Pick a weekend vibe" took 22s average — the longest question. Consider simplifying options
  • Only 62 outbound clicks to occasions page — add clearer CTAs on the result screen
Key takeaways for next campaign
  • Plan a coordinated Instagram Story push at launch — the viral spike proved it works
  • Reduce quiz to 4–5 questions max to improve start-to-complete rate
  • Build sharing into the result screen (pre-filled IG Story template, WhatsApp deep link)
  • Add a clear CTA on the result page linking to catering or menu pages
  • Consider running the quiz during a festive period for higher engagement
